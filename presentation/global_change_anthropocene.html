    • Global Change
      • Formal definition
        • Set of alterations in the functioning of the Earth system and in human societies caused by human activity. It results from scaling up impacts at the local scale. We have become aware that the accumulation of local impacts generates alterations on a global scale. These global impacts threaten the livelihoods of the societies that create them.
          • Change is happening rapidly (in the last 200 years).
          • It is not caused by an external agent, but by a species on the planet.
